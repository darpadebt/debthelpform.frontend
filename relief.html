Which Debt Relief Path Is Right for You?

There’s no one-size-fits-all answer. We’ll help you compare the pros and cons so you can move forward confidently.

✅ Debt Settlement

Negotiate with creditors to reduce what you owe. Works well for larger unsecured balances or significant hardship.

  • Potentially lower overall balances
  • Single, program-based payment
  • Short-term credit impact possible

✅ Debt Consolidation

Combine debts into one payment, often at a lower rate.

  • Best with fair-to-good credit
  • One predictable monthly payment
  • Streamlines repayment

✅ Hardship Plans

Short-term payment relief for qualifying circumstances like medical issues or job loss.

  • Reduce or pause payments temporarily
  • May have limited credit impact

✅ Bankruptcy (Last Resort)

Eliminates or reorganizes debt but has long-term credit tradeoffs. Consider only after exploring other options.

  • Chapter 7 or Chapter 13
  • Immediate stop to collections
